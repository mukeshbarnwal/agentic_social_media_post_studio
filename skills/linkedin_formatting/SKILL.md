# LinkedIn formatting

- Open with a one-line hook under ~120 characters when possible.
- Use short paragraphs (1–3 lines) with intentional whitespace for feed readability.
- Hashtags: 3–5 relevant tags; avoid stuffing; no spaces inside a tag.
- Close with a clear CTA (comment, DM, download, event link) consistent with the brief.
- For carousels, keep each slide caption complementary to the slide title; avoid repeating the hook verbatim.
