# Brand voice

- Match the selected tone: formal stays third-person and measured; casual allows contractions; punchy uses short sentences and strong verbs.
- Avoid hype adjectives unless the research chunks justify them with a cited claim.
- Prefer concrete nouns and numbers pulled from sources over generic platitudes.
- Do not invent credentials, metrics, employers, or dates that are not present in `source_markers`.
